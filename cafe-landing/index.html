The Quantum Threat

Why Quantum Computing Breaks Current Security

🔓

Cryptographic Vulnerability

Shor's and Grover's algorithms threaten to break RSA and ECDSA, the cryptographic foundations protecting today's digital world—from internet communications to blockchain networks.

⚡

Transit Attacks

Once you sign a transaction, your public key becomes visible on-chain and could be targeted for quantum attacks during transaction propagation and confirmation.

💾

Storage Attacks

Previously exposed keys on the blockchain remain vulnerable. Quantum computers could retroactively derive private keys from public keys already recorded on-chain.

The Urgency is Real

The transition to quantum-resistant cryptography (PQC) has become an urgent global priority. Organizations must act now to preserve the confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity of digital assets before Q-Day arrives.
